MENTAL HEALTH CHECK


Lets grow together
  • ZenMinds believes assessments can be a valuable first step toward getting treatment.
  • If you're 16 or over, this self-assessment quiz can help you better understand how you've been feeling recently.
  • With each question, think about how you've been feeling over the last 2-3 weeks.
  • This quiz is NOT a diagnostic tool.
  • Mental health disorders can only be diagnosed by a licensed mental health provider or doctor.
  • People often stop from seeking help, out of fear that their concerns aren't legitimate or severe enough to warrant professional intervention.
  • Below is a list of 12 questions designed to help you determine if you might be experiencing depression or anxiety.
  • The questions relate to life experiences common among people who have depression.
  • Please read each question carefully, and indicate how often you have experienced the same or similar challenges in the past few weeks.
